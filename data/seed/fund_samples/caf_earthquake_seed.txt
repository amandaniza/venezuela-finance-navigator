CAF announces Earthquake Reconstruction Seed Fund for Venezuela

Caracas / Caracas regional office — The Development Bank of Latin America and
the Caribbean (CAF) today announced a new grant facility, the CAF Earthquake
Reconstruction Seed Fund, to support urgent reconstruction priorities following
recent seismic damage in coastal Venezuela.

The fund will provide grant capital focused on healthcare facility stabilization,
critical infrastructure repair, and emergency housing. Eligible activities include
hospital repair, rubble removal adjacent to public clinics, and water sanitation
works in affected municipalities.

Capital type: Grant
Target sectors: Healthcare, Infrastructure, Housing
