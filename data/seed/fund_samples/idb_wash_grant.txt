IDB launches WASH Emergency Response Grant for Venezuela disaster recovery

The Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) announced a WASH Emergency Response
Grant to finance water and sanitation restoration and public-health infrastructure
in disaster-affected communities.

The grant supports water sanitation systems, hospital water supply, and related
healthcare infection-control works. Commercial debt is not part of this facility.

Capital type: Grant
Target sectors: WASH, Healthcare
